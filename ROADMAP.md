# Public Roadmap (High Level)

Last updated: 2026-03-13

## Tier 1: Stability and Hardening

- Continue reliability hardening for media and edge-case runtime failures.
- Complete governance consistency for permissions and collaboration behavior.
- Keep deployment and rollback safety controls continuously verified.

## Tier 2: Platform and Workflow

- Improve CI/workflow maintainability and least-privilege defaults.
- Strengthen documentation governance and planning hygiene.
- Extend observability and operational verification coverage.

## Tier 3: Net-New Capabilities

- Expand messaging/collaboration features after Tier 1/2 remain stable.
- Broaden mission contribution and community/economy feature set.
- Add user-facing operational transparency surfaces (for example status summaries).

This roadmap is intentionally high-level; detailed plans remain private.
